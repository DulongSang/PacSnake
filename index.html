Game Rules:

Pacsnake rules:
Character: Pacman, Pacghost, Pacsnake, dots(food)
User: Pacman VS Pacsnake
Each member has 100 marks initially

Pacman version:
Pacman hits Pacsnake: Wins
Pacman hits white Pacghost (stop for 2 seconds)
Pacman hits blue Pacghost (deduct 10 marks)
Pacman hits yellow Pacghost (deduct 50 marks)
Pacman hits red Pacghost: Lose

Pacsnake version:
Pacsnake hits Pacman: Lose
Pacsnake hits white pacghost (speed up)
Pacsnake hits blue Pacghost (receive 10 marks)
Pacsnake hits yellow Pacghost( receive 50 marks)
Pacsnake hits red Pacghost (double longness)

Food dots:
BIG dots: 5 marks each
Small dots: 1 marks each
Win or Lose:
Decide by marks in limit time
